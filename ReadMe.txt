StockBandit
===========

A tool that monitors stocks (once registered) matching them against specific analysis models. Upon a movement to suggest a Buy or Sell, the system sends out email alerts.

Currently supports:

Bollinger Bands
Volume
MACD

Author: Lewis Harvey (me@lewisjharvey.co.uk)
